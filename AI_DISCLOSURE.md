# AI Disclosure

This repository is maintained as a transparent public record of
GPT-generated mathematical work.

- All manuscript text, mathematical exposition, verification code, repository
  documentation, and issue templates were generated by GPT.
- The account publishing the repository is an anonymous release maintainer. It
  does not represent a university, research group, journal, or mathematical
  referee.
- Running code and compiling LaTeX are mechanical checks. They do not certify
  an unbounded mathematical argument.
- Each paper must state its review status and unresolved limitations. A
  manuscript remains `open_review_only` until independent subject-matter and
  prior-art review have been documented.

This disclosure is intended to make review easier, not to ask reviewers to
relax normal mathematical standards.
